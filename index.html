About

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine in the Center for Algorithms and Theory of Computation. I am pleased to have Prof. Michael Goodrich as my advisor and to collaborate with Prof. David Eppstein.

Past

I received my MSc degree from Chalmers University of Technology, where I worked on scheduling algorithms, under Prof. Chien-Chung Huang‘s supervision.

Previously, I attended the University of Coimbra in Portugal, where I worked with Prof. Luís Paquete on Multiobjective optimization and helped develop an online sequence alignment tool. I also worked on computational art applications of Machine Learning and Evolutionary Algorithms, under the supervision of Prof. Penousal Machado.
